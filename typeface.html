Typeface

This was one of the first assignments that I got in my first semester at uni, it was to create a typeface. The typeface didnt necessarily have to a font that people ould write with, it could be anything aslong as it was legible

The idea behind my design started with me wanting to create a simple and obscure typeface, not something you would naturally see someone writing an essay with. Something more out there that you would see on a poster or maybe CD art. I tried to use just lines, triangles and circles, And each line at an angle is (with maybe a few exceptions) is at a 45° angle. The reason behind all of this is because i wanted to keep the font consistent. Everything looks better when its kept consistent.

I’m happy with the way the typeface turned out, some of the letters took longer to do like the N, took a while (and some second opinions) to get it right. I would like to try and do the lower case alphabet in the same style sometime as well.
