About me

Timeline

Hi there, my names Jordan Gault and i'm currently studying Interaction design at Ulster University in Belfast. It hasn't been that long since I was introduced to this type of design, just a few years ago I had no idea what I wanted to do career wise.

When I was in school I was thinking of going down a ICT based career path, which wasn't something I was exited about or anything, it was just the only thing I could think of. But once I left school I went to Northern Regional College and instead of doing the ICT course that I was thinking of doing I picked Interactive media, which was all about design.

This course was eye opening for me, it showed me that you can actually make a career out of designing things, rather than sitting behind a computer all day typing out mind numbing code.

Now I'm studying Interaction design at Ulster University in Belfast and I am Constantly expanding my deisgn knowledge.
